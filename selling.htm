To sell an item on this site, you need to create a seller’s account. You will have to provide your information and contacts. Registration is free, but you will pay to list your item(s) on this website. The payment rates are of two categories :
 (1) Indepedent Account, and (2) Premium Account.

Independent Account is small business owners, or individuals with few item to list. While the Premuim Account is for persons with large stock of items. For more benefit of the various accounts go to registrations.
We value your privacy. Therefore we do not share the billing information you have provided on this site with anyone. Hence, your information is secure and protected with SSL technology.

How do I receive payment when my goods are purchased on this website?


Sellers will receive payments for commodities sold on nairatags.com on transfer of the item(s) to the buyers and submission of a shipping proof (tracking information)to nairatags.com delivery services.

Sellers are required to provide the following on registration.

  1. Bank Account information (for payment)
  2. Proof of item ownership

Sellers authenthicity will be done by nairatags.com as process of ensuring trust. However, you are expected to verify and updated your profile whenever there is a change in details you have provided.
